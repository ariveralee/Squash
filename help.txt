
usage: squash [command] [--args]

Here are the common commands for squash:

adding mongo credentials)
squash add-cred --alias <myalias> --url <connectionurl>

removing mongo credentials)
squash del-cred --alias <myalias> --url <connectionurl>

adding user to mongo database)
squash add-user --use <alias> --user <username> --pass <password> --db <database> --roles <role1,role2>

getting help)
squash --help
